Embark on a journey to obtain your Class B Commercial Driver's License (CDL) - your passport to driving a range of commercial vehicles like straight trucks and large buses. Let's steer through what you need to know for getting your Class B CDL.

<Snippet file="class-b-diagram.mdx" />

## Quick Facts

| **Aspect** | **Details** |
|------------|-------------|
| **License Type** | Class B CDL |
| **Vehicle Types** | Straight trucks, large passenger buses, segmented buses |
| **GVWR Requirement** | 26,001 pounds or more, towing vehicles not exceeding 10,000 pounds |
| **Ideal For** | City bus drivers, dump truck drivers, delivery truck drivers |
| **Training Focus** | Vehicle handling and safety for Class B vehicles |
| **Skills Test Components** | Vehicle inspection, basic control skills, driving test |
| **Employment Opportunities** | Diverse roles in local delivery, passenger transport, and specialized services |

## Overview

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Understanding Class B CDL">
    A Class B CDL permits you to drive commercial vehicles with a Gross Vehicle Weight Rating (GVWR) exceeding 26,001 pounds, or towing vehicles not over 10,000 pounds GVWR.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Diverse Career Opportunities">
    This license opens doors to jobs like city bus driving, dump truck operating, and delivery truck driving.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## How to Get a Class B CDL

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check Your Eligibility">
    Confirm you meet your state's age, medical, and legal criteria.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Acquire the Learner's Permit">
    Clear the [general knowledge test](/cdl/essentials/written-tests/general-knowledge) to secure your [learner's permit](/cdl/cdl-types/cdl-permit).
  </Step>

  <Step title="Engage in Specialized Training">
    Enroll in a CDL school focusing on Class B vehicle operations.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Master the Skills Test">
    Successfully navigate the [skills test](/cdl/essentials/skills-test) including vehicle inspection, basic controls, and road driving.
  </Step>
</Steps>
