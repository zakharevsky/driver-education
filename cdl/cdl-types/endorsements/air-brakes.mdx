## Demystifying the Air Brakes Restriction

<Tip>**Need a quick overview?** If your [CDL skills test](/essentials/skills-test) was in a vehicle without air brakes, an <code>Air Brakes Restriction</code> is added to your CDL, barring you from driving vehicles with air brake systems.</Tip>

## Air Brakes Restriction at a Glance

| **Aspect** | **Details** |
|------------|-------------|
| **What It Is** | Restriction added if CDL test was in a non-air brake vehicle. |
| **Impact** | Limits you to vehicles without air brakes, affecting job options. |
| **Removing the Restriction** | Requires passing a [knowledge test](/essentials/written-tests/air-brakes) and [skills test](/essentials/skills-test) in an air brake-equipped vehicle. |
| **Benefits of Removal** | Expands eligible vehicle types and job opportunities. |

For CDL holders, understanding the Air Brakes Restriction is crucial. This guide breaks down what this restriction means and its impact on your driving capabilities.

## Understanding the Impact of the Air Brakes Restriction

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Vehicle Type Limitation" icon="ban">
    This restriction confines you to driving commercial vehicles without air brakes, limiting the scope of your job prospects.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Employment Opportunities" icon="briefcase">
    As many larger trucks and buses use air brakes, having this restriction can narrow your employability in these sectors.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## How to Lift the Air Brakes Restriction

<Steps>
  <Step title="Knowledge Test on Air Brakes">
    Prepare for and pass an air brakes written test at your local DMV.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Skill Test in an Air Brake-Equipped Vehicle">
    Successfully complete a CDL skills test in a vehicle with air brakes.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Update Your CDL">
    After passing the tests, get the restriction removed from your CDL.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Frequently Asked Questions About Air Brakes Restriction

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Is removing the restriction challenging?">
    While it demands some study and practice, the process is manageable, especially for those with experience in commercial vehicles.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I drive any air brake-equipped vehicle post-removal?">
    Yes, removing the restriction legally permits you to operate commercial vehicles fitted with air brake systems.
  </Accordion>
