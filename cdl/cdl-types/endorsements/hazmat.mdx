## HazMat Endorsement: Quick Reference Table

| **Requirement** | **Details** |
|-----------------|-------------|
| **Knowledge Test** | Focus on safety, emergency response, and HazMat regulations. |
| **TSA Background Check** | Required for all HazMat drivers to assess security risks. |
| **Documentation** | Proof of citizenship or legal presence in the U.S. |
| **Fees** | Applicable for both the knowledge test and TSA background check. |
| **Renewal Period** | Every five years, including a new TSA check and knowledge retest. |
| **Career Impact** | Access to specialized freight opportunities and potential for higher pay. |

## HazMat Endorsement Explained

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Defining the HazMat Endorsement">
    This endorsement verifies that you're trained to transport hazardous materials, adhering to strict safety standards and regulations.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="The Importance of HazMat Training">
    Due to the increased risks of hauling hazardous materials, this endorsement is crucial for ensuring driver proficiency in handling such cargo.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Earning Your HazMat Endorsement

<Steps>
  <Step title="Study and Pass the Knowledge Test">
    Prepare for and pass a [written exam focused on hazardous materials transport](/essentials/written-tests/hazmat), including safety, emergency responses, and legal guidelines.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete TSA Background Screening">
    Undergo a [Transportation Security Administration (TSA) background check](essentials/tsa-background-check), mandatory for all HazMat drivers.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Submit Required Documents">
    Provide necessary documents, such as proof of citizenship or legal status, to your state's DMV.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Cover the Fees">
    Be prepared to [pay fees](/fees-costs) for both the knowledge test and TSA background check.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Common Queries on HazMat Endorsement

  <Accordion title="TSA Check Duration">

  <Accordion title="Endorsement Renewal">
