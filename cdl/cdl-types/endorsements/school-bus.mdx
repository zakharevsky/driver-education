## Quick Reference: School Bus Endorsement

| **Requirement** | **Details** |
|-----------------|-------------|
| **Knowledge Test Focus** | School bus operation, student safety protocols, emergency responses. |
| **Skills Test Requirement** | Practical driving and student management in a school bus. |
| **Additional State Requirements** | May include background checks, specialized certifications. |
| **Vehicle Types Covered** | All sizes of school buses (subject to state rules). |
| **Renewal** | Periodic renewal needed, often aligned with CDL renewal. |
| **Career Opportunities** | Qualifies for school bus driving roles, ensuring child safety. |

## Acquiring the School Bus Endorsement

<Steps>
  <Step title="Knowledge Test on School Bus Operations">
    Prepare for and pass [a knowledge test covering the unique aspects of school bus operation](/essentials/written-tests/school-bus), focusing on student safety and emergency response.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Skills Test in a School Bus">
    Demonstrate your [practical skills](/essentials/skills-test) in a school bus, including handling the vehicle and managing young passengers.
  </Step>

  <Step title="State-Specific Requirements">
    Fulfill any additional criteria set by your state, such as background checks or extra certifications.
  </Step>
</Steps>
