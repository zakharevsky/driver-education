## What is Tanker Endorsement?
Tanker endorsement is an official authorization added to a commercial driver's license (CDL). This endorsement **allows the holder to legally transport large volumes of liquid and gas in tank vehicles**. It is required for drivers who operate commercial tank trucks.

<Tip>[Free Tanker practice tests & exam information](/cdl/essentials/written-tests/tankers) </Tip>

## At a Glance

| **Requirement** | **Details** |
|-----------------|-------------|
| **Knowledge Test** | Focus on safe operation of tank vehicles and liquid dynamics. |
| **Additional Endorsements** | HazMat may be needed for hazardous liquid transport. |
| **Gallon Threshold** | Endorsement required for vehicles carrying 1,000 gallons or more. |
| **Water Hauling** | Under 1,000 gallons can be hauled without the endorsement. |
| **Safety Protocols** | Emphasis on leak checks and securement of tanks. |

## The Essentials

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Understanding the Tanker Endorsement">
    The "N" endorsement on your CDL qualifies you to **transport bulk liquids and gases**, crucial for drivers handling materials like water, milk, or liquid sugar, which require specific safety measures.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="The Need for a Tanker Endorsement">
    This endorsement is key for driving jobs in sectors involving bulk transport of liquids or gases, demanding specialized handling skills due to their potential hazards.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Criteria for Requiring a Tanker Endorsement">
    If you're operating a commercial vehicle designed to haul 1,000 gallons or more of liquid or gas, this endorsement is a must, even if the vehicle isn't always loaded to capacity.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## How Do I Get a Tank Endorsement for my CDL?

<Steps>
  <Step title="Knowledge Test Preparation">
    Begin with CDL training that focuses on tanker operations. Study topics essential for the [tanker endorsement knowledge test](/cdl/essentials/written-tests/tankers), such as safe driving of tank vehicles and the physics of liquid movement.
  </Step>

  <Step title="State-Specific Requirements">
    Some states may have extra criteria, like the [HazMat endorsement](/cdl/cdl-types/endorsements/hazmat) for transporting hazardous liquids.
  </Step>
</Steps>
