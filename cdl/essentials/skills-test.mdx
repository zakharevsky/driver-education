<Tip>**In a hurry? Quick summary:** After passing your [CDL written tests](/essentials/written-tests/overview), the next challenge is the CDL Skills Test. This practical exam evaluates your ability to handle a commercial vehicle in various situations.</Tip>

## Components of the CDL Skills Test

The CDL Skills Test is divided into three parts: the **Pre-Trip Inspection**, the **Basic Vehicle Control**, and the **On-Road Driving test**. Each section is designed to assess different competencies essential for safe and effective commercial driving.

<AccordionGroup>

<Accordion title="Pre-Trip Inspection">
You will be required to demonstrate a thorough pre-trip inspection of your vehicle to ensure it is safe to drive. This includes checking the engine compartment, brake system, fuel system, and more.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Basic Vehicle Control">
This portion tests your ability to control the vehicle. You will be asked to perform maneuvers such as backing up, parallel parking, and alley docking.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="On-Road Driving">
The on-road test assesses your practical driving skills in a variety of traffic situations, including highway, city, and rural driving.
</Accordion>

</AccordionGroup>

## Preparing for the Skills Test

Preparation for the Skills Test should be hands-on and as close to the actual test conditions as possible.

<AccordionGroup>

<Accordion title="Practice with a Qualified Instructor">
Enroll in a training program with a qualified instructor who can provide guidance and feedback on your driving techniques.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Familiarize Yourself with the Test Route">
If possible, familiarize yourself with the area where the skills test will be conducted to reduce surprises on test day.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Understand the Scoring Criteria">
Know what the examiners are looking for during the test. Make sure you understand the scoring criteria and practice accordingly.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Visualize the Maneuvers">
Use visualization techniques to mentally practice the maneuvers you'll be required to perform, building muscle memory and confidence.
</Accordion>

</AccordionGroup>

## Tips for Test Day

On the day of your Skills Test, keep these tips in mind:

<AccordionGroup>

<Accordion title="Conduct a Thorough Pre-Trip Inspection">
Take your time during the pre-trip inspection and verbalize what you are checking to demonstrate your knowledge to the examiner.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Stay Calm During Vehicle Control Tests">
During the Basic Vehicle Control tests, stay calm and remember the techniques you've practiced. If you make a mistake, don't panic; focus on completing the maneuver safely.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Drive Defensively on the Road Test">
For the On-Road Driving test, prioritize safety. Use defensive driving techniques, obey all traffic laws, and communicate your actions clearly.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Review and Rest Beforehand">
Review the key points of the test the night before, and get plenty of rest. Arrive at the test location early to have ample time to prepare mentally.
</Accordion>

</AccordionGroup>

The Skills Test is your opportunity to demonstrate the practical skills you've developed. With dedicated practice and a clear understanding of what's expected, you'll be well-equipped to pass the test and earn your CDL.
