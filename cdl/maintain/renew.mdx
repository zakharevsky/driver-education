## Steps for Renewal

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check Expiration">
    Be aware of your CDL expiration date and start the renewal process well in advance.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Gather Documentation">
    Collect all necessary documents needed for the renewal process.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Any Required Tests">
    Some states may require a vision test or other assessments for renewal.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit Your Application">
    Apply for renewal either online, by mail, or in person at your local DMV.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pay Renewal Fees">
    Ensure you pay any associated renewal fees to complete the process.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Remember, keeping your CDL valid is not just a legal requirement but also a professional responsibility. Stay ahead of the renewal process to ensure uninterrupted career progress.
