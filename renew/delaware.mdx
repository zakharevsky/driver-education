## Replace or Renew Your Driver License in Delaware

### In Person
You may renew your license up to 180 days before it expires. The reminder will be sent to you by mail about 60 days before the expiration date. Note that there is a $10 late fee for renewals after the expiration date. You can renew your license in person at [any DMV office](https://driving-tests.org/dmv-near-me/delaware-dmv-office-locations/) without an appointment.

#### Plan of action
<Steps>
  <Step title="Step 1">
    Fill out and submit an application form at a DMV office.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 2">
    Bring the following:
    - Your current driver license
    - Proof of residency (if you moved)
    
    You may also be asked to verify your Social Security Number and legal presence in the U.S.
    Use this [interactive guide](https://www.dmv.de.gov/documentguide/index.shtml) to choose the right documentation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 3">
    Pass an eye-screening test.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 4">
    Pay the fee of 40 dollars by cash, check or card. Pay the late fee of $10 if applicable.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Your new license will be issued for 8 years, with the exception of immigration length of stay and Driving Privilege Card holders.  
[Find a DMV office near you](https://driving-tests.org/dmv-near-me/)

### By Mail
You may renew your license by mail only if you must be out of the state because you are in the military, an employee of the Department of Defense, or a dependent of someone who is.

To renew your license by mail, you must provide the following information and items to the DMV:
1. Your full name.
2. Your date of birth.
3. Your Social Security number.
4. Your driver license number if known.
5. Your current Delaware address to be placed on your new license. (If you no longer have a residence in Delaware, you may supply the address of a family member.)
6. Your mailing address.
7. A contact phone number or email address.
8. A copy of the military assignment orders for where you are currently stationed or a copy of your Military ID card or Department of Defense/Geneva Conventions ID card.
9. Your signature.
10. A check or money order, payable to DMV for $40.00.
11. A self-addressed, stamped envelope (for faster service).

Send all the above information and items to one of the following addresses:

For overnight delivery via FedEx or UPS (not the United States Postal Service):
Division of Motor Vehicles  
Attn: Driver Services Department  
303 Transportation Circle  
Dover, DE 19901

For delivery via regular mail:
Division of Motor Vehicles  
Attn: Driver Services Department  
P.O. Box 698  
Dover, DE 19903

### Online
You are eligible to renew online if:
1. You are over 18 years of age
2. You haven't lost your driving privileges
3. You didn't use the self-service kiosk at your last DMV visit
