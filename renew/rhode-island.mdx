## Replace or Renew Your Driver License in Rhode Island

### In Person
You must renew your RI driver license before it expires. You can renew your operator's driver license in person at one of the [DMV locations](http://www.dmv.ri.gov/locations/index.php#1). You must renew in person if one of the following is true about you:
1. You are a non-US citizen
2. Your license expired more than 180 days ago
3. Your license is suspended or revoked

#### Plan of Action
<Steps>
  <Step title="Step 1">
    Complete the application form. You can download it [here](http://www.dmv.ri.gov/documents/forms/license/LI-1.pdf).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 2">
    Bring the form and your current license. If your license is lost, check the third page of the application for the list of documents required. If you are a visa applicant, you are required to bring immigration/naturalization documents.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 3">
    Pay the fee of 61.50 for the 5-year renewal. You can pay by cash, money order, checks, and credit card (MasterCard, Discover, Visa, and American Express). You will have to add the $1.50 technology surcharge and a service fee of 1.50 dollars if paid by a credit card.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 4">
    Receive your temporary license.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 5">
    You will receive your permanent license by mail within 45 days. If you don't receive the license after 45 days, call (401) 462-5862.
  </Step>
</Steps>

[Find a DMV office near you](https://driving-tests.org/dmv-near-me/)

### By Mail
Renewal by mail is not offered at the moment.

### Online
If you need to change the address, you will have to do it [here](https://www.ri.gov/DMV/address_change) before proceeding to renewal.

To renew online, access the [Official State of Rhode Island Online Service](https://www.ri.gov/DMV/licenserenewal/).

#### Plan of Action
<Steps>
  <Step title="Step 1">
    Complete your application online.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 2">
    Pay the fee of $61.50 by card.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 3">
    Your license renewal request will be processed within 3 business days.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 4">
    Your new license will be sent to the address on file within 60 days.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Note that online renewal may be unavailable on Saturday 8:30 PM through Monday 7:30 AM and every day between 6:45 AM and 7:30 AM.
