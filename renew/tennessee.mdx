## Renew Your Driver License in Tennessee

### In Person

Your Tennessee driver's license expires every 8 years. You may renew in person at any [Driver Services Center](https://driving-tests.org/dmv-near-me/tennessee-dmv-office-locations/).

**Plan of Action:**

1. **Visit a Driver Services Center**: Find a location using [this map](https://driving-tests.org/dmv-near-me/).
2. **Bring documentation**: Current license, proof of citizenship, SSN, two proofs of residency.
3. **Pass the vision test**.
4. **Pay the fee**: $28 for an 8-year license.
[Find a DMV office near you](https://driving-tests.org/dmv-near-me/)

***

### Online

Renew online at [TN.gov](https://www.tn.gov/safety/driver-services/online.html) if eligible.

***

### By Mail

Available for military members stationed out of state.
