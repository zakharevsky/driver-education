## Renew Your Driver License in Utah

### In Person

Renew at any [Driver License Division office](https://driving-tests.org/dmv-near-me/utah-dmv-office-locations/).

**Plan of Action:**

1. **Visit a DLD office**: Use [this map](https://driving-tests.org/dmv-near-me/).
2. 2. **Bring documentation**: License, ID, SSN, residency proof.
3. 3. **Pass the vision test**.
4. 4. **Pay the fee**: $52 for a 5-year license.
[Find a DMV office near you](https://driving-tests.org/dmv-near-me/)

***

### Online

Renew online at [Utah DLD](https://dld.utah.gov/).

***

### By Mail

Available for military stationed out of state.
