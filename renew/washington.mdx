## Renew Your Driver License in Washington

### In Person

Renew at any [Washington DOL office](https://driving-tests.org/dmv-near-me/washington-dmv-office-locations/).

**Plan of Action:**

1. Visit a DOL office using [this map](https://driving-tests.org/dmv-near-me/).
2. 2. Bring documentation: License, ID, SSN, residency proof.
3. 3. Pass the vision test.
4. 4. Pay the fee: $72 for a 6-year license.
[Find a DOL office near you](https://driving-tests.org/dmv-near-me/)

***

### Online

Renew online at [Washington DOL](https://www.dol.wa.gov/).

***

### By Mail

Available for eligible drivers.
