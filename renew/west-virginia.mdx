## Renew Your Driver License in West Virginia

### In Person

Renew at any [West Virginia DMV office](https://driving-tests.org/dmv-near-me/west-virginia-dmv-office-locations/).

**Plan of Action:**

1. Visit a DMV office using [this map](https://driving-tests.org/dmv-near-me/).
2. 2. Bring documentation: License, ID, SSN, residency proof.
3. 3. Pass the vision test.
4. 4. Pay the fee: $10 for a 5-year license.
[Find a DMV office near you](https://driving-tests.org/dmv-near-me/)

***

### Online

Renew online at [West Virginia DMV](https://transportation.wv.gov/DMV/).

***

### By Mail

Available for eligible drivers.
