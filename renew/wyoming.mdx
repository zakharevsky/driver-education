## Renew Your Driver License in Wyoming

### In Person

Renew at any [Wyoming DMV office](https://driving-tests.org/dmv-near-me/wyoming-dmv-office-locations/).

**Plan of Action:**

1. Visit a DMV office using [this map](https://driving-tests.org/dmv-near-me/).
2. 2. Bring documentation: License, ID, SSN, residency proof.
3. 3. Pass the vision test.
4. 4. Pay the fee: $30 for a 4-year license.
[Find a DMV office near you](https://driving-tests.org/dmv-near-me/)

***

### Online

Renew online at [Wyoming DOT](https://www.dot.state.wy.us/).

***

### By Mail

Available for eligible drivers.
