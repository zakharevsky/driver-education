## Tips for Test Day

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Before the Test">
    Make sure to get a good night's sleep and review your notes, especially the tricky parts. A clear mind makes for a confident test-taker.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="During the Test">
    Read each question carefully. Don't rush, and remember to breathe. Use the process of elimination for multiple-choice questions.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="After the Test">
    Whether you ace it or need a retake, reflect on the experience. There's always room for improvement, and every attempt is a step forward.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

  <Card title="Track Your Progress" icon="chart-bar">
